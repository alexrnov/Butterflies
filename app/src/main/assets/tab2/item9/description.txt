Papilio epiphorbas is a butterfly of the family Papilionidae. It is found in Madagascar and the Comoro Islands.

Papilio epiphorbas is a member of the oribazus species-group. The clade members are: Papilio oribazus Boisduval, 1836; Papilio epiphorbas Boisduval, 1833; Papilio nobilis Rogenhofer, 1891
